What is it?

This is project developped in the context of Msc Digital Media Engineering at DTU

The goal is to provide a deep understanding of a corpus of texts. For each text, you can observe the topic distribution, and see the most similar documents.

How it works?

Each node represents a text. Clic on it and drag it to move them. When you hover a node, connections to other text are highlighted. The left pannel which contains information about the text is also updated.

In the left panel, you can clic on the red button to acces to a deeper analysis of the text.
